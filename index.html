About Us

Who can join the AIT Club?

Any student at SVCE can join the AIT Club.

What is the AIT Club?

The AIT Club is a student-run organization at Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering that aims to promote the field of information technology and provide its members with opportunities to learn, grow, and network.

What are the benefits of joining the AIT Club?

Access to a variety of resources, such as books, journals, and software.
